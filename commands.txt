```
simpleca ca list
simpleca ca init [--name name] [$certargs]
simpleca ca delete [--name name]
simpleca ca show [--name name] // shows pub of ca
simpleca ca show [--name name] [--include-privkey]

simpleca cert list [--ca name]
simpleca cert sign [--ca name] [$certargs] {certname}
simpleca cert delete [--ca name] {certname}

simpleca cert show [--ca name] [--include-privkey] {certname} // shows pub, optionall private of cert

Example Usage:

simpleca init

simpleca cert sign etcd1 1.2.3.4
simpleca cert sign etcd2 1.2.3.4 --san 4.3.2.1 --san 8.8.8.8
simpleca cert sign etcd3 --ca my-ca 8.8.8.8 --san localhost --san google.com

simpleca cert delete --ca my-ca etcd3 

```
